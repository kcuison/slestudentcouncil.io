Welcome to Freehold High School!

Mission: The FHS Student Council is the umbrella organization for other school activities. It promotes the general welfare of the student body; establishes higher standards of leadership, scholarship, character, service, and school spirit; and improves students-faculty-administration communications. Meetings are for members only.

2019 Marching Band Festival

The festival serves to highlight the tremendous amount of preparation and rehearsal that students and teachers apply to each half time show. When considered collectively, thousands of hours are spent writing, rewriting, studying, rehearsing and performing the shows performed by each band. The range and scope of each show is distinct and varied, providing the districtâ€™s students with a wide array of musical opportunities. The instrumental music programs of all six high schools are part of both the curricular and the co-curricular hours of the school day. The time spent in band serves not only to educate in music and related subject areas, but to showcase talent and teach character, respect, and dedication.

2019 Spooktacular (3rd Place Winner!)

The tradition of transforming downtown Freehold into a haunted hotspot continued this year with art students from the Freehold Regional High School District decorating the windows of local businesses as a part of the annual Spooktacular Window Painting Contest on Oct. 17.
